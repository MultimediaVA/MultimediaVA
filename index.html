Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions clients ask about my video editing, eCommerce, and virtual assistance services.

1. What types of video editing styles do you offer?

I specialize in YouTube content (reaction, documentary, vlog, and talking-head), social media reels, short-form TikToks, and branded edits. I work with tools like Adobe Premiere, DaVinci Resolve, and CapCut Pro.

2. Can you handle eCommerce tasks like product listings?

Yes. I’ve listed over 100+ products for Shopify and Amazon brands. I can handle title optimization, image formatting, descriptions, tags, and basic SEO to improve visibility and conversions.

3. What tools and platforms are you experienced with?

I use Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, CapCut Pro, Canva, OBS, Google Workspace, Trello, Slack, Zoom, and eCommerce platforms like Shopify, Amazon Seller Central, and product research tools.

4. How do you communicate and collaborate with remote clients?

I’m available via email, Skype, and Zoom, and I use tools like Google Drive, Trello, and Slack to manage revisions, share files, and communicate updates in real-time.

5. How quickly can you deliver a video edit or listing task?

Turnaround time depends on the project, but most short-form edits are delivered within 24–48 hours. Product listings and administrative tasks typically take 1–2 days per batch.
